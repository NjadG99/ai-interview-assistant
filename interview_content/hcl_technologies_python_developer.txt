7. Q: What are the two major loop statements?

8. Q: What are the built-in types available in Python?
   A: Python uses five numeric types: Booleans, integers, long integers, floating-point numbers, and complex numbers. Except for Booleans, all numeric objects are signed. All numeric types are immutable. Booleans are represented by two values: True and False.
